Advance Freight provides New Zealand-wide courier and freight services. Our transport and delivery options include parcel delivery, same-day and overnight services, freight forwarding, taillift and hiab.

Tell Me More

Bulk freight Service

Bulk freight orders direct to the customer or to multiple customers. Available New Zealand-wide, including the Auckland metro area. Rate structures are built around volume and destination logistics.

Courier Service

Single or multiple parcel delivery, including return products, relocations or redirects, urgent courier, and airfreight (some exclusions apply). Available for both domestic and international deliveries. We offer annual rate contracts, on-time delivery and assistance with cost saving methods to pack and move products.

Logistics-Integrated Management

We have key relationships with other providers to allow us to offer cost effective solutions and logistic efficiencies. Your products will be delivered with care and on time.
